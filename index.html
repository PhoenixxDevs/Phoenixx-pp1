Bio

Independent songwriter, producer and rapper. Usually presented in a jazz-funk hip hop style, eccentric flows and unexpected lyrics are the essence of the Phoenixx music experience.
Listening to a Phoenixx instrumental you will find unusual sample manipulation, atmospheric sound design and unconventional section changes, all brought together by drum beats that make your head nod.
As a drummer in the band Finding Gaz, he provides a funky breakbeat style of drumming that drives the bands syncopated and quirky rhythms.

Influences

Phoenixx's slightly odd hip hop style is heavily influenced by artists such as Gorillaz, MF DOOM and Del the Funky Homosapien to name just a few. However his rhythmical and melodical delivery is more akin to artists such as Mac Miller, Mick Jenkins, JID and Ocean Wisdom.
Musical muses that inspire his songwriting thought process include the likes of Jamiroquai, Thundercat, Anderson .Paak, Vulfpeck and The Mouse Outfit.

Interests

Although music is a big part of Phoenixx's life, it isn't (always) everything.
During downtime Phoenixx enjoys playing video games (Rocket League, Lethal Company, Satisfactory + waaaay more).
He also enjoys writing code. Mainly proficient in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, using them to make primitive games and other whacky tools, he also has some familiarity with C (pp and #), Python and Kotlin.
